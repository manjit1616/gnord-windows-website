Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about GNORD thermal break aluminium windows and doors for residential and commercial projects.

What are thermal break aluminium windows?

Thermal break aluminium windows use an insulating barrier inside the aluminium profile. This barrier reduces heat transfer through the frame and helps improve indoor comfort.

What are the main benefits of thermal break windows and doors?

The main benefits are thermal insulation, sound insulation, noise reduction, better comfort in hot and cold weather, and lower demand on heating and air-conditioning systems.

Can GNORD products be used for both homes and commercial buildings?

Yes. GNORD thermal break aluminium windows and doors are designed for residential and commercial applications, including villas, residential complexes, offices, hotels, mixed-use buildings, and high-street shops.

Do the windows help in both hot and cold climates?

Yes. In hot weather, thermal break profiles help reduce heat transfer from outside to inside. In cold weather, they help reduce indoor heat loss through the aluminium frame.
