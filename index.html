Thermal Break Aluminium Profile Glass Windows and Doors

GNORD Windows designs and supplies aluminium profile glass windows and doors that improve thermal insulation, reduce noise, and support energy-efficient buildings in hot, cold, and noisy environments.

Request a Quote View Window & Door Solutions

Sound Insulation, Noise Reduction, and Thermal Insulation

Thermal break aluminium windows use an insulating barrier inside the profile to reduce heat transfer through the frame. The result is a more comfortable indoor environment and better control over heating and air-conditioning costs.

  • Designed for hot climates, cold climates, and high-noise locations.
  • Supports double glazing and aluminium profile window systems.
  • Suitable for villas, apartments, offices, hotels, and street-facing commercial projects.

Energy Saving Performance Compared with Standard Aluminium Windows

Thermal break windows help reduce unwanted heat gain and heat loss. In the existing GNORD content, the product is positioned as saving up to 66% compared with a standard aluminium window by reducing heating and air-conditioning demand.

Effective in All Weather Conditions

On a hot day, thermally broken frames inhibit heat transfer from outside to inside. On a cold day, they help keep valuable indoor heat from leaking outdoors.

Hot Weather Comfort

Thermal break profiles help rooms stay cooler by reducing conductive heat transfer through aluminium frames.

Cold Weather Protection

The insulating barrier helps protect indoor warmth and can reduce the cold-touch feeling often found on standard aluminium frames.

Noise Reduction

Combined with suitable glazing, GNORD window systems support quieter indoor spaces for homes, hotels, offices, and street-facing properties.
