Sound Insulation, Noise Reduction, and Thermal Insulation

Thermal break aluminium windows use an insulating barrier inside the profile to reduce heat transfer through the frame. The result is a more comfortable indoor environment and better control over heating and air-conditioning costs.

  • Designed for hot climates, cold climates, and high-noise locations.
  • Supports double glazing and aluminium profile window systems.
  • Suitable for villas, apartments, offices, hotels, and street-facing commercial projects.

How Do Thermal Breaks Work?

In thermally broken construction, the interior and exterior sides are joined by an insulated barrier that is designed to inhibit conductive thermal energy loss. This means that heat and cold cannot easily travel through the frame. So, in summer, a thermal break will prevent the blazing heat from venturing into your home. And in winter, a thermal break will prevent the warm air inside your home from escaping.

Thermal break windows and doors help keep the interior glass and frame temperature closer to the room temperature, improving comfort and reducing condensation risk. Some indoor condensation can still occur, but exterior condensation often shows that the window is sealing out outdoor temperature and humidity effectively.
